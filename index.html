Education Roadmap

Essential milestones for the Academic Year.

Discovery Phase

Years 7-9

In KS3, the goal is to discover where your natural logic and creativity overlap. This is the foundation for your GCSE selections.

Strategy
  • Master Algebra and Ratio (Foundation for Science)
  • Aim for a "Reading Age" higher than your actual age
  • Join at least one STEM or Debating club
Discovery Portfolio

Start saving your best projects in Tech, Art, or English. This builds the confidence needed for high-pressure Year 11 exams.

GCSE & Level 2

Triple Science and Mathematics results determine your access to STEM careers in and beyond.

STEM Priority

Grade 7+ in Maths is the typical passport for AI or Engineering pathways.

Rule of 9

Aim for quality over quantity. 8-10 strong GCSEs are better than 12 weak ones.

Post-16 Mastery

Years 12-13

Specialization begins. Choose the pathway that matches how you learn: academic theory (A-Levels) or practical immersion (T-Levels).

Path A: Academic

Study 3-4 A-Levels. Best for Research, Medicine, and Law. Focus on UCAS points.

Path B: Vocational

Choose a T-Level or Level 3 Apprenticeship. Best for immediate earning potential.

Decision Year
